Information Technology for Development

Monday, August 6th , 2018

Information Technology for Development (IT4D) is an international conference organized by my college – Deerwalk Institute of technology in association with University of Windsor, Canada. The conference is organized every year and serving as a platform for the enthusiastic tech guys to show case their ideas which will lead the world in the path of development. With many international and national dignitaries, IT4D has become one of the must attend event for the people involved in technology.
